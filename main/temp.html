PESIT South ACM Student Chapter was started in 2007 by the Computer Science Department of PESIT South Campus. We also organise workshops, seminars and a couple of amazing events like inGenius (annual) and the Pursuit (bi-annual Online Treasure Hunt)!

</comp-soc>

<about-us>

inGenius, the flagship event of PESIT South ACM, is an annual 24-hour hackathon.

After three successful iterations, inGenius 2015 is back! This time around, our theme is Open Hack. The future is data-driven. So get behind the wheel & hack it out on the 2nd & 3rd of October, 2015.

</about-us>

Thinking big? Think bigger! Come and blow our minds.
